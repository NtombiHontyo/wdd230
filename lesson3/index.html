Site Purpose

The purpose of the Durban Chamber of Commerce site is to provide its members with a website to view all the relevant details about the chamber as well an opportunity for potential members to join. It also gives them an opportunity to interact more extensively about their own business interests and offers available within their subscription. It will provide the visitors of the site with an in-depth tour of the history of the City of Durban in terms of trade and industry. Both members and potential members will leave the site with their questions answered about the business demographics of Durban.

The Website Target Market

Target audience

The audience of the Durban Chamber of Commerce website is anyone who has an interest in growing their business. Growing this business by getting funds or growing the business by marketing the business. Whatever it may be; the website will help the viewer expand their entrepreneurial skills within the city of Durban.

Personas

Persona 1: A person who has a business in Durban and would like to expand his/her customer pool. The site will provide them with relevant training on how to do that and link them with effective advertising as well as let them know of upcoming events such as luncheons where they can network.

Persona 2: A company that is looking into relocating their company to Durban. They may look at the site to get the type of demographics that are in Durban in terms of what his company may need

Scenarios

  • A business man looking into investing in a thriving business may visit the site to ascertain which business are thriving in the area.
  • A student studying business at a local university in Durban may want to have a look at a more practical approach of what he/she maybe studying and visit the site to find out about any upcoming events and additional trainings.
  • A local business owner may visit the site to see what benefits are available as member of the chamber in order to help his business thrive.
  • A member looking into getting certain documentation such as ‘Certificate of Origin’ may visit the site to get the details of the staff member to get hold of to obtain such documentation.
  • • A young person wanting to start a business may visit the site to see what they may offer him to boost his young tender business.
